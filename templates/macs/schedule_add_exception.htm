Add a new schedule exception

Exceptions override the daily schedule by either allowing the Makerspace to be open when it might normally be closed (such as on a school hoilday or during a vacation week), or can be used to close the Makerspace when it would normally be open to accomodate a special event.

The "Start time" and "End time" must be entered in 24-hour format (e.g. 7:00, 16:30, 18:00, etc).
